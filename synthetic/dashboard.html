Cohort Retention
% of each signup cohort still placing orders N months after signup, averaged across all cohorts.
Average Retention Curve (Month 0–6)
Reading it: Month 0 (signup month itself, partial period) is naturally lower than Month 1 — the first full month after signup is peak engagement, after which retention decays and stabilizes around ~32% by Month 6. This is the natural point to trigger win-back campaigns, before further decay sets in.
